Intern Photographer

Part-time / Contract On-site Project Based / Stipend

Job Description

We need an eye for detail. If you can make a coffee cup look like a luxury item, we want you. As an Intern Photographer, you will be responsible for capturing high-quality images for our clients' products, events, and marketing campaigns. You will work closely with our Art Directors to bring visual concepts to life.

Key Responsibilities

  • Capture high-quality product photography and lifestyle shots.
  • Cover corporate events and social gatherings for clients.
  • Assist in setting up lighting and equipment for shoots.
  • Edit and retouch photos using Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.
  • Maintain and organize the photo library.

Required Skills

  • Proficiency with DSLR/Mirrorless cameras and lighting equipment.
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating product and event photography skills.
  • Expertise in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Lightroom).
  • Good eye for composition, color, and lighting.
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience with diverse clients.
  • Access to professional equipment.
  • Networking opportunities within the industry.
  • Competitive stipend and project bonuses.
